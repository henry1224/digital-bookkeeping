# Module PRD: Reporting

## Goal

Provide decision-ready dashboards and analysis for cashflow, inventory, and financial reports.

## Reports

1. Executive Dashboard
2. Laba Rugi
3. Neraca
4. Trial Balance
5. Buku Besar
6. Cash Position
7. Daily Cash Movement
8. Payment Commitment
9. Stock Valuation
10. Stock Variance
11. Waste & Loss
12. Reconcile HPP (Fase 2)
13. Flash Cost (Fase 2)

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Reports filter by outlet and period.
2. Reports can export PDF and Excel.
3. Financial reports drill down to journal/source transaction.
4. Inventory reports reconcile to stock balance.
5. Cash reports reconcile to bank book.
